What is Ideate: Breaking the Barriers? üß†

Ideate: Breaking the Barriers is a Ideathon conducted by IEEE MANIT Student Branch under its event called SAMWAD: Women in Engineering. SAMWAD is particularly focused on working with, and for Women in Engineering. The problem statements for the Ideathon are focused on some genuine problems faced by women in their daily course of life like Women Safety, Right to education in remote areas, and Women empowerment and employment.

The aim of conducting this Ideathon is to promote Women in Engineering and to nudge people towards ideating and implementing innovative solutions to tackle the problems faced by women.

Questions? Answers.

Who all are eligible to participate in the Ideathon?

Any undergraduate student, irrespective of location in India, their college, branch, language or gender can participate.

Can only girls / women participate?

No, any student irrespective of the gender they identify themselves as, is allowed to participate. The problem statements are related to women, but anybody with an idea can participate.

What is the maximum number of participants in a team allowed?

A minimum of an individual and maximum of four members are allowed to be in a team.

How do we submit our ideas?

Submissions are required to be made on the Devfolio platform. The teams are encouraged to submit a video presenting a demo of what they have built along with the link to the GitHub repository and the hosted link (optional).

Is it necessary to host your solution?

- No, a video explanation is necessary for the presentation, and if they decide to implement something, they have the choice to include hosting or GitHub as additional components.

What is the last date of submission?

The deadline of submission is Oct 29, 2023 at 11:59 PM (Video submissions). Adhere to the deadline strictly. Any delay will not be considered for the evaluation.

What will be the judging criteria?

You will be judged based on the innovation of your idea and also on what you've built and its practical use. A complete and clear Idea presentation is one of the many factors you will be judged on. Other judging criteria are subject to IEEE MSB‚Äôs concern and are not disclosed.

Do I need to pay any money to register for the Ideathon?
